**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**  
**Dhaka, Bangladesh — January 10, 2026**

RKPulse, the first artist-led global music platform from Bangladesh, officially launches under the solo vision of founder **Rubel Khandokar** — an independent artist, songwriter, composer, and cross-platform technologist.

Built entirely by the founder, RKPulse blends creativity, technology, and business to empower fans and creators worldwide. The platform introduces a new era of artist-led earning logic, fan-powered payouts, and modular transparency — all crafted with founder-grade polish and strategic privacy.

> “RKPulse is not just a platform — it’s my living legacy. Every line of code, every lyric, every banner reflects the pain, pride, and purpose of building something truly ours.”  
> — *Rubel Khandokar, Founder*

RKPulse stands as a defiant response to industry gatekeeping, offering fans direct participation and creators full control. With a solo-founder identity and global discoverability at its core, RKPulse is ready to redefine how music platforms are built — and who gets to build them.
