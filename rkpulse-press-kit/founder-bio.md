# Founder Bio

**Rubel Khandokar** is an independent artist, songwriter, composer, and cross-platform technologist from Bangladesh.  
He is the solo founder of RKPulse — the first artist-led global music platform from Bangladesh, blending creativity, technology, and business to empower fans and creators worldwide.

Rubel’s journey is rooted in emotional storytelling, poetic songwriting, and technical excellence.  
He treats every bio, banner, and commit message as part of his living legacy.

RKPulse is not just a platform — it’s a movement led by a visionary who believes in solo-founder identity, strategic privacy, and global discoverability.
